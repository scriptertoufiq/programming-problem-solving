Convert Decimal to Binary
    1.Divide the number by 2
    2.Take the remainder
    3.Divide the quotient by 2
    4.Take the remainder
    5.Repeat the process until the quotient is 0
    6.Write the remainders in reverse order
    7.The remainders are the binary number

Convert Binary to Decimal
    1.Take the binary number
    2.Multiply the rightmost digit by 2^0
    3.Multiply the next digit by 2^1
    4.Multiply the next digit by 2^2
    5.Repeat the process until all the digits are multiplied
    6.Add all the products
    7.The sum is the decimal number
